assert
Entity: predicate

asserta
Entity: predicate

Usage:
assert(Clause)
asserta(Clause)

Appends a clause to the run-time database. (asserta appends in the beginning)
 
 
Clause  the clause to append. 

Fails if Clause is not a clause.
 

Example:

?-  assert(test(1,2)), test(X,Y), write(X), write(" "), write(Y), nl.
