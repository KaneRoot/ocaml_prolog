The Lottery
 

We also have a lottery for our customers who bought a subscription before the release of version 2.0. Five randomly selected of them will win. The chance will be bigger for those who bought earlier. Your chance will be proportional to the number ALL+1-N , where ALL is the number of all people who bought a subscription and N is your number. This means that the person who buys first enjoys twice the bigger chance to win over that in the middle. And the person at the end has minimal chance (almost zero).

The prize which they will share is 10% of all money collected from subscription buying until the release of Version 2.0.

An additional prize will be granted to one of those who have bought a subscription before the release of version 1.0. This prize will be 2% of all money collected from subscription buying before the release of version 2.0.

The winners will have the opportunity to choose to take the money or to go for a different experience, like for example, a trip to the Bulgarian seaside.

All free licenses which are given by contest or by any other reason do not give the right to participate in the lottery.
